About Our Store

Welcome to ComputerStore, your number one source for all things tech. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of computers and accessories, with a focus on quality, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2010 by Alex Johnson, ComputerStore has come a long way from its beginnings in a home office. When Alex first started out, his passion for providing the best equipment drove him to do intense research and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the world and are thrilled to be a part of the tech industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality tech products that combine performance with value pricing, while establishing a successful relationship with our customers and our suppliers. We continually strive to meet or exceed our customer's needs and expectations of price, service, and selection.

Our Team

Our team is made up of experienced and knowledgeable professionals who are passionate about technology and customer service. We are dedicated to helping you find the perfect products for your needs and ensuring that you have the best possible shopping experience.
